
[VGSH](![[Pasted image 20260118101416.png]]): Vanguard Short-Term Treasury ETF
- VGSH is a short term government bound fund. SGOV is very short-term government bonds. VGSH pays a slightly higher rate. They are both interest bearing funds designed for safe short term money parking that will earn 4%. Their share price shouldn't move much. These funds earn about the same rate as inflation. So they really don't grow your money. To grow your money you want a dividend about double the rate of inflation, or you put money in growth fund.
