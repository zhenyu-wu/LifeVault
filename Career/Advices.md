#### How to Win People Over
- Seek status is not a bad thing, it is a normal human need. But we need to do it in the right way. The key is to achieve the two dimensions: (1) assertiveness and (2) caring for others at the same time. 
- When promoting yourself, don't forget to praise others. 
- Help others in the way that is unique to you, form bonds with others that are unique to you. 
- Promote yourself but in some humorous way.
